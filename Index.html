Bell Tent Glamping

4.5 (151)
Netley · ~10 min drive

Set inside Royal Victoria Country Park on the water. A mix of bell tents and wooden cabins, with on-site staff and space for events — guests often mention the warm welcome and how well kept the grounds are.

Solent Haven Glamping

5.0 (10)
Warsash · ~20 min drive

Converted shipping-container cabins and an Airstream, right on the Solent waterfront. Guests come back for the views of passing ships, private decking, and the ability to swim from the shore at high tide. Very few units — book early.

Beechen Glamping

4.9 (23)
Near Bishop's Waltham · ~25 min drive

Lodges scattered across a working farm, with open views over fields and grazing animals. A short walk gets you to a village pub. Popular with families and small weddings alike.

Fancy Camping

5.0 (12)
Cadnam · ~25 min drive

Spacious bell tents with proper beds, right on the edge of the New Forest — deer regularly wander across the field. Hand-built showers, a well-equipped kitchen tent, and a pub within walking distance.

Paultons Campsite & Glamping

4.8 (210)
Ower, near Romsey · ~25 min drive

A well-run, spotless site right by the M27 — the obvious pick if you're combining a stay with Paultons Park or Peppa Pig World. Easy access, clear pitches, and hands-on owners.
